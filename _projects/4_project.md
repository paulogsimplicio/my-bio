As part of the Brazilian team at the University of São Paulo, I co-developed a ground robot designed for autonomous biodiversity sampling in challenging tropical rainforest environments.

**Core contributions:**
- Designed and built a ruggedized mobile robot capable of collecting leaf-litter and soil samples in unstructured, GPS-degraded environments.
- Deployed and field-tested in campaigns in Brazil and Singapore.
- Collaborated with biologists from Brazil and France on species identification and biodiversity analysis pipelines.

**Result:** International Finalist — *XPRIZE Rainforest Competition*, 2024.
