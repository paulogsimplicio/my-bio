This project addresses autonomous mission planning for photogrammetry-based 3D reconstruction of large structures (dams) using a commercial UAV.

**Core contributions:**
- Coverage planner that enforces ground sampling distance (GSD), image-overlap percentage, row-spacing, and view-geometry constraints simultaneously.
- Generates optimized flight paths that maximize reconstruction quality while minimizing flight time.
- Validated on real dam sites; produces point clouds and meshes suitable for structural integrity assessment.

**Published at:** *ICUAS 2024*, Chania, Greece — P. V. G. Simplicio and G. A. S. Pereira.
